About Tadz Bakes

Tadz Bakes is a student-founded baking brand created in 2024 with a passion for homemade treats and a dream to bring sweet memories to every celebration. What started as a side hustle at university quickly grew into a go-to name for affordable, delicious, and beautifully-packaged treats loved by people all over Botswana. We specialize in cupcakes, brownies, cakes, and cinnamon rolls — all made fresh with love and attention to detail. Whether you’re ordering for a birthday, a small event, or simply treating yourself, we offer something special to make every bite memorable.

At Tadz Bakes, we believe in quality, creativity, and customer happiness. We also love sharing baking tips and recipes within our community to inspire others to explore the joy of baking.
